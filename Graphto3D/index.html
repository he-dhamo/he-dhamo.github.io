Graph-to-3D: End-to-End Generation and Manipulation of 3D Scenes using Scene Graphs (ICCV 2021)

Helisa Dhamo *     Fabian Manhardt *     Nassir Navab     Federico Tombari    

Technical University of Munich    Google

* The first two authors contributed equally.




We propose a fully generative model to synthesize novel 3D scenes from a scene graph. The same model is capable of scene manipulations. Our method essentially predicts object-level 3D bounding boxes together with appropriate 3D shapes, which are then combined to create a full 3D scene.
